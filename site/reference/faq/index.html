    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)¶

    General Questions¶

    What is GacetaChat?¶

    GacetaChat is an AI-powered platform that helps users search, analyze, and understand documents from Costa Rica's official gazette (La Gaceta). It combines natural language processing with extensive document archives to provide intelligent answers to questions about regulations, procedures, and government publications.

    How does GacetaChat work?¶

    GacetaChat uses advanced AI technology to: 1. Index Documents: Automatically download and process PDF documents from La Gaceta 2. Create Embeddings: Convert document text into searchable vector representations 3. Understand Queries: Process natural language questions in Spanish 4. Find Relevant Content: Match queries with the most relevant document sections 5. Generate Answers: Provide contextual responses based on official sources

    Is GacetaChat free to use?¶

    GacetaChat offers a free tier with limited features (10 searches per month) and several paid subscription plans with additional features and higher usage limits. See our pricing page for detailed information.

    What languages does GacetaChat support?¶

    GacetaChat primarily supports Spanish, as it's designed for Costa Rican government documents. Limited English support is available for interface elements and basic queries.

    How current is the information in GacetaChat?¶

    GacetaChat automatically downloads and processes new documents from La Gaceta daily. Most new publications are available for search within 24-48 hours of their official publication.

    Technical Questions¶

    How accurate are the search results?¶

    GacetaChat uses state-of-the-art AI models to achieve high accuracy in document retrieval and answer generation. However, users should always verify important information by consulting the original documents or legal professionals.

    Can I access the original documents?¶

    Yes, GacetaChat provides links to the original PDF documents for all search results. You can view, download, and share the source documents directly.

    What file formats are supported?¶

    GacetaChat primarily processes PDF documents from La Gaceta. The platform can export search results and documents in various formats including PDF, Word, Excel, and JSON.

    Is there an API available?¶

    Yes, GacetaChat offers API access for Professional and Enterprise plan subscribers. The API allows programmatic access to search functionality and document retrieval.

    How do I integrate GacetaChat with my existing systems?¶

    GacetaChat provides REST APIs, webhooks, and export capabilities. Contact our support team for assistance with specific integration requirements.

    Account and Billing¶

    How do I create an account?¶

    1. Visit the GacetaChat website
    2. Click "Sign Up"
    3. Enter your email address and create a password
    4. Verify your email address
    5. Complete your profile information

    How do I upgrade my subscription?¶

    1. Log in to your account
    2. Go to "Account Settings"
    3. Click "Upgrade Plan"
    4. Select your desired plan
    5. Complete the payment process

    What payment methods are accepted?¶

    GacetaChat accepts major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers.

    Can I cancel my subscription anytime?¶

    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

    Do you offer refunds?¶

    We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after cancellation.

    Usage and Features¶

    How many searches can I perform?¶

    Search limits depend on your subscription plan: - Free: 10 searches per month - Basic: 100 searches per month - Professional: 1,000 searches per month - Enterprise: Unlimited searches

    What types of documents can I search?¶

    GacetaChat includes all document types published in La Gaceta, including: - Decretos (Decrees) - Acuerdos (Agreements) - Resoluciones (Resolutions) - Circulares (Circulars) - Avisos (Notices) - Concursos (Contests) - Licitaciones (Tenders)

    Can I set up alerts for new documents?¶

    Yes, Professional and Enterprise subscribers can set up automated alerts for: - New documents matching specific keywords - Updates to particular topics or regulations - Deadline reminders - Custom search criteria

    How do I save my searches?¶

    1. Perform a search
    2. Click "Save Search" in the results area
    3. Give your search a descriptive name
    4. Access saved searches from your profile

    Can I share documents with my team?¶

    Yes, GacetaChat offers collaboration features for team accounts: - Share documents via secure links - Create team workspaces - Collaborate on document analysis - Track team activity

    Data and Privacy¶

    How is my data protected?¶

    GacetaChat implements enterprise-grade security measures: - Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest - Access Controls: Role-based permissions and authentication - Compliance: GDPR and privacy regulation compliance - Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

    Do you store my search history?¶

    Yes, we store your search history to improve your experience and provide features like saved searches and recommendations. You can delete your search history at any time through your account settings.

    Can I export my data?¶

    Yes, you can export your search history, saved documents, and other account data. Contact support for assistance with data export requests.

    Is my information shared with third parties?¶

    We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the platform, always under strict confidentiality agreements.

    Troubleshooting¶

    Why am I not getting relevant results?¶

    Try these strategies: - Use more specific keywords - Check spelling and try alternative terms - Adjust date range filters - Try different question formulations - Include more context in your query

    The platform is running slowly. What should I do?¶

    • Clear your browser cache
    • Try using a different browser
    • Check your internet connection
    • Simplify complex queries
    • Contact support if issues persist

    I forgot my password. How do I reset it?¶

    1. Go to the login page
    2. Click "Forgot Password"
    3. Enter your email address
    4. Check your email for reset instructions
    5. Follow the link to create a new password

    Why can't I access certain features?¶

    Feature access depends on your subscription plan. Some features like API access, unlimited searches, and team collaboration are only available to Professional and Enterprise subscribers.

    I'm having trouble with document downloads. What should I do?¶

    • Check your internet connection
    • Try downloading smaller files first
    • Clear your browser cache
    • Disable browser extensions temporarily
    • Contact support if issues persist

    Legal and Compliance¶

    Can I use GacetaChat for legal advice?¶

    GacetaChat provides information and document search capabilities but does not provide legal advice. Always consult qualified legal professionals for legal interpretation and advice.

    Are the documents in GacetaChat legally binding?¶

    GacetaChat provides access to official government documents, but users should always verify information by consulting the original sources and current regulations.

    How do I cite documents found through GacetaChat?¶

    Include the following information in your citations: - Document title and number - Publication date - La Gaceta edition number - Access date - GacetaChat URL (if applicable)

    What if I find outdated or incorrect information?¶

    Report any issues with document accuracy or currency to our support team. We regularly update our document index and will investigate reported issues.
