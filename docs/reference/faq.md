# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is GacetaChat?
GacetaChat is an AI-powered platform that helps users search, analyze, and understand documents from Costa Rica's official gazette (La Gaceta). It combines natural language processing with extensive document archives to provide intelligent answers to questions about regulations, procedures, and government publications.

### How does GacetaChat work?
GacetaChat uses advanced AI technology to:
1. **Index Documents**: Automatically download and process PDF documents from La Gaceta
2. **Create Embeddings**: Convert document text into searchable vector representations
3. **Understand Queries**: Process natural language questions in Spanish
4. **Find Relevant Content**: Match queries with the most relevant document sections
5. **Generate Answers**: Provide contextual responses based on official sources

### Is GacetaChat free to use?
GacetaChat offers a free tier with limited features (10 searches per month) and several paid subscription plans with additional features and higher usage limits. See our pricing page for detailed information.

### What languages does GacetaChat support?
GacetaChat primarily supports Spanish, as it's designed for Costa Rican government documents. Limited English support is available for interface elements and basic queries.

### How current is the information in GacetaChat?
GacetaChat automatically downloads and processes new documents from La Gaceta daily. Most new publications are available for search within 24-48 hours of their official publication.

## Technical Questions

### How accurate are the search results?
GacetaChat uses state-of-the-art AI models to achieve high accuracy in document retrieval and answer generation. However, users should always verify important information by consulting the original documents or legal professionals.

### Can I access the original documents?
Yes, GacetaChat provides links to the original PDF documents for all search results. You can view, download, and share the source documents directly.

### What file formats are supported?
GacetaChat primarily processes PDF documents from La Gaceta. The platform can export search results and documents in various formats including PDF, Word, Excel, and JSON.

### Is there an API available?
Yes, GacetaChat offers API access for Professional and Enterprise plan subscribers. The API allows programmatic access to search functionality and document retrieval.

### How do I integrate GacetaChat with my existing systems?
GacetaChat provides REST APIs, webhooks, and export capabilities. Contact our support team for assistance with specific integration requirements.

## Legal and Compliance

### Can I use GacetaChat for legal advice?
GacetaChat provides information and document search capabilities but does not provide legal advice. Always consult qualified legal professionals for legal interpretation and advice.

### Are the documents in GacetaChat legally binding?
GacetaChat provides access to official government documents, but users should always verify information by consulting the original sources and current regulations.

### How do I cite documents found through GacetaChat?
Include the following information in your citations:

- Publication date
- La Gaceta edition number
- Access date
- GacetaChat URL (if applicable)

Report any issues with document accuracy or currency to our support team. We regularly update our document index and will investigate reported issues.
